Dr. Chairi Kiourt - Principal Researcher of Artificial Intteligence - Applications

Dr. Chairi Kiourt is Principal Researcher at the ATHENA - Research and Innovation Centre in Information, Communication and Knowledge Technologies since 2019. He holds a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering (2007), an M.Sc. in Systems Engineering and Management (specializing in Information and Communication Systems Management) (2010), and a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence (2017). Dr. Kiourt has contributed to numerous national and European research projects and is co-author of multiple peer-reviewed articles and conference papers in the areas of collective artificial intelligence, multi-agent systems, reinforcement learning, and XR technologie.
